This is a visualization depicting the trends of terrorist attacks world wide from 1970 to 2017. Double click on a country in order to gain a more detailed view and use the two handles of the slider to select a specific time frame to examine. Additional charts can be accessed through the link in the upper right corner labeled "supplementary charts". Please note that the data may take a few seconds to fully load on first initiation.
